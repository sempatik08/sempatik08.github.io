Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

StoryTime is a personal, single-user tool operated by its owner to create short storytelling videos and upload them to the owner's own social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram). It is not offered as a service to the public.

Information We Collect

StoryTime does not collect, store, or process any personal information from third parties. The only account data accessed is the owner's own TikTok account information (basic profile) and the ability to upload videos to the owner's own account, via TikTok's official APIs, after explicit authorization.

How Information Is Used

Access tokens are stored locally on the owner's personal computer and are used solely to upload videos created by the tool to the owner's own accounts. No data is shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on the owner's personal device and can be deleted at any time by removing the locally stored files. API access can be revoked at any time from the connected account's security settings.

Third-Party Services

StoryTime uses official platform APIs (TikTok, Instagram/Meta) subject to their own terms and privacy policies.
